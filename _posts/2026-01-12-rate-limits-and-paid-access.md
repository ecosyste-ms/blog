**In order to cover the cost of operating and maintaining ecosyste.ms we’re introducing rate limits and paid API access tiers.**

Three months ago [we announced that we would be introducing rate limits](https://blog.ecosyste.ms/2025/09/01/rate-limiting-the-right-way.html) to help cover the cost of maintaining ecosyste.ms. Today we’re introducing those rate limits alongside paid-for tiers for users building and scaling services based on ecosyste.ms data. 

Users can make up to 300 calls per hour across all ecosyste.ms services combined, and we continue to operate a polite pool of users who will receive priority during peak periods. 

If you need to make more calls per hour you can purchase increased rate limits through our self-service process available at [ecosyste.ms/pricing](http://ecossyte.ms/pricing). From there you can register, select, and pay using most payment methods. From there you can generate and manage API access tokens to be used with any of ecosyste.ms' services.

If you require an invoice, a commercial licence, or higher rate limits: get in touch with us at [hello@ecosyste.ms](mailto:hello@ecosyste.ms).
